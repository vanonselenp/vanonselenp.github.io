If you only read one thing, [here is my primer on what I have learnt about Agentic Engineering.](https://www.petervanonselen.com/agentic-engineering/).

If you have just landed here, this is the entry route I would suggest. Pick the line that sounds most like you.

## I am sceptical of AI hype but I use the tools daily

Start with [From AI Skeptic to Constant Collaborator](/2025/10/20/what-i-have-learnt/) — the long version of how I changed my mind. Then [Money for Nothing](/2026/06/17/money-for-nothing/) for the why behind the discipline, and [Encode It, Don't Remember It](/2026/06/07/encode-it-dont-remember-it/) for what changes about the day job.

## I lead engineers and I am trying to separate real change from theatre

Start with [The more an AI can break, the less you let it do](/2026/06/02/i-am-sorry-dave/) — a production incident with the agent kept deliberately at arm's length. Then [Why You Shouldn't Speedrun a Production Refactor](/2025/12/12/speedrunning-prod-refactors/) on what the easy version of "let AI do it" actually costs. Finally [The Best Part Has No AI in It](/2026/05/18/best-part-has-no-ai-in-it/) on where the leverage really is.

## I want the game-dev story start to finish

Start with [Sky Islands](/2025/08/21/sky-islands/) — the original board game prototype, before any of this grew legs. Then walk forward chronologically from the [game-development tag](/tags/game-development/). [Finally... A Wild MVP Appears](/2025/12/04/finally-a-wild-mvp-appears/) is the three-month milestone.

## I want to see what AI in production actually looks like

The [case studies tag](/tags/case-study/) has the named-incident posts: the trading engine that ran for forty-five minutes, the GDPR-shaped basket table, the 280-file refactor that became unshippable, the OpenSearch cache nobody on the team understood.

## I want to practise this stuff

[How I Learnt to Stop Worrying and Love Agentic Katas](/2026/03/05/learn-to-love-agentic-coding/) is the entry point. The [agentic-katas](https://github.com/vanonselenp/agentic-katas) repo has the exercises themselves.
